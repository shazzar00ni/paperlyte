**Last Updated:** September 2024

## Overview

Paperlyte is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable privacy laws. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## What Data We Collect

### Current Version (MVP)

- **Waitlist Information:** Email address, name, and interest category (when you join our waitlist)
- **Local Usage Data:** Notes and settings stored locally on your device only
- **Analytics:** Basic usage statistics stored locally (no personal data transmitted)

### Future Versions (Q4 2025+)

- **Account Information:** Email, display name, profile preferences
- **Encrypted Notes:** Your notes, encrypted end-to-end before storage
- **Usage Analytics:** Anonymized performance and feature usage data

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on:

- **Consent:** For waitlist signup and marketing communications
- **Legitimate Interest:** For essential app functionality and security
- **Contractual Necessity:** For providing cloud sync services (future)

## How We Use Your Data

### Current Usage

- **Waitlist Management:** To notify you about product launches and updates
- **App Functionality:** To provide local note-taking capabilities
- **Service Improvement:** To understand how users interact with the app (locally only)

### Future Usage (With Your Consent)

- **Cloud Sync:** To synchronize your encrypted notes across devices
- **Account Management:** To provide personalized app experience
- **Security:** To protect your account and detect unauthorized access

## Data Storage and Security

### Current Implementation

- **Local Storage Only:** All your notes remain on your device
- **No Server Storage:** We don't store your personal notes on our servers
- **Waitlist Data:** Stored securely with industry-standard encryption

### Future Implementation

- **End-to-End Encryption:** Your notes are encrypted before leaving your device
- **Zero-Knowledge Architecture:** We cannot read your encrypted data
- **Secure Infrastructure:** SOC 2 compliant hosting with advanced security measures

## Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### Right to Access

- Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
- Contact: privacy@paperlyte.com

### Right to Rectification

- Correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data
- Update your information directly in the app or contact us

### Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

- Request deletion of your personal data
- **Current:** Email us to remove you from the waitlist
- **Future:** Use the "Delete Account" feature in app settings

### Right to Data Portability

- Receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Current:** Waitlist data can be provided in JSON/CSV format
- **Future:** Export all notes in standard formats (Markdown, JSON, CSV)

### Right to Restrict Processing

- Limit how we process your personal data
- Contact privacy@paperlyte.com

### Right to Object

- Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- Opt out of marketing communications at any time

### Right to Withdraw Consent

- Withdraw consent for any processing based on consent
- Use unsubscribe links in emails or contact us directly

## Account Deletion and Data Export

### Current Process

**Waitlist Removal:**

1. Email privacy@paperlyte.com with "Delete My Data" in subject
2. Include the email address used for waitlist signup
3. We'll confirm deletion within 7 days

**Local Data:**

- Clear your browser's localStorage to remove all local app data
- No server-side data to delete (local-only storage)

### Future Process (Q4 2025+)

**Account Deletion:**

1. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
2. Confirm deletion and download data export if needed
3. Account and all data deleted within 30 days

**Data Export:**

1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Export Data
2. Choose format: Markdown, JSON, CSV, or PDF
3. Download complete archive of your data
